WHY DO MOST WEBSITES END WITH ' .com '?

=>URL BREAKDOWN
Lets first breakdown the URL (Uniform Resource Locator) for reference purposes.

https://whatis.techtarget.com/glossaries

-1st part of URL = PROTOCOL (https://) This part of the URL specifies which type of protocol is being used(HTTP in this case)
-2nd part of the URL = DOMAIN (whatis.techtarget.com) Identifies which server we are requesting the document from.
-3rd part of the URL = PATH (glossaries) Identifies the specific document or resource we are interested in.

=>MEANING OF .com
.com is derived from the word commercial, indicating its original intended purpose for domains registered by commercial organizations.

=>MAIN REASONS AS TO WHY SITES PREFER TO USE .com
- It's the oldest and most recognisable domain extension
- It's one of the most famous TLD(Top Level Domain)

=>WHAT IS TLD?
-A top-level domain (TLD) is one of the domains at the highest level in the hierarchical Domain Name System of the Internet.
- A few examples of the same are:

.com (short for dot commercial)
.org (short for dot organization)
.int (short for dot international)
.edn (short for dot education) ,etc.

-TLD's usually have a reason as to why they are named the way they are.
- Some TLD's are named based on the geographical area where they were made in(example: .in-> India, .io->British Indian Ocean Territory) and sometimes, they're named based on their purpose (example: .biz for business)
